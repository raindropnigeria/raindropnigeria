About Raindrop

Raindrop Nigeria is a company dedicated to transforming agriculture by building the storage, logistics, and supply chain infrastructure farmers and businesses need. By solving problems of spoilage, poor transport, and unreliable distribution, Raindrop ensures produce moves smoothly from farms to consumers. Its vision is to create a seamless system that connects production with people, making agriculture more sustainable and reliable in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Services

Storage Solutions
Raindrop provides proper storage systems to help farmers preserve their produce after harvest. This reduces post-harvest losses and maintains the quality of goods until they are ready for distribution.
Logistics & Distribution
The company builds and manages transportation networks to move goods efficiently from farms to markets, restaurants, retailers, and even families. This helps solve the problem of poor roads and unreliable transport.
Supply Chain Infrastructure
Raindrop creates the “backbone” for agriculture by connecting every step in the supply chain—farmers, warehouses, logistics, and buyers—ensuring produce reaches the right place at the right time.
Quality Assurance
Through controlled systems for storage and movement, Raindrop ensures that agricultural products stay fresh and retain their value, helping farmers and businesses deliver quality goods to consumers.
Business Enablement Platforms
Raindrop's infrastructure supports other businesses, such as food delivery platforms, retailers, and restaurants, by making it easier for them to source and deliver agricultural products reliably.
In summary, Raindrop offers end-to-end agricultural support—from storage and logistics to building full supply chain systems—helping farmers, businesses, and consumers overcome the barriers that limit Nigeria's agricultural growth.

Our Vision at Raindrop

W're reimagining agriculture in Nigeria building a seamless supply chain that connects farmers straight to consumers, cuts down waste, and maximizes efficiency. With world-class storage, a powerful logistics network, and smart technology, we're making sure fresh produce moves fast and stays in top condition from farm to table.

Our mission is bigger than moving food. We're empowering farmers to grow more without fear of spoilage, giving businesses reliable access to quality goods, and ensuring households never have to worry about where their next supply will come from.

Raindrop is laying the foundation for a bold new era in agriculture an ecosystem where innovation, technology, and trust fuel growth, sustainability, and opportunity for generations to come.
